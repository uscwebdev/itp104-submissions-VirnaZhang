Final Project Proposal

by Virna Zhang

Topic

The website will be showcasing a brunch cafe. The primary goal for the brunch cafe page is to create a enticing brand that will represent the themes and goals of the cafe itself, but also be visually-appealing to a consumer standpoint.

Audience

My intended audience for this website are potential customers who are interested in visitng a casual brunch spot and are curious about what the brunch cafe has to offer.

Design, Color, and Scheme

In terms of the color schemes, I am hoping to use more warm and pastel colors. I will primarily be aiming to incorporate more greens and browns as a way to bring out the earthy tones and forest essence that the brunch cafe is inspired by.

Design Inspirations

  • Arden Cafe
  • Harucake
  • Cafe Spot LA

Scope

There will be three total pages for this website. The brunch cafe restaurant website will feature a homepage that displays the restaruant and cafe's atmosphere and theme, a second page will feature information about the menu and drinks, and the third page is the reservation form page which will allow for customers to fill out a form and reserve a spot at the brunch cafe.
